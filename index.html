Blue

You can make a button blue simply by adding the "blue" class to any a tag or button tag. The other normal options are available for this tag as well. Click on any of the buttons below to view example code:

Blue Blue - Heavy Blue - Large Blue - Mini Flat

Blue - Default

You can make a button blue by adding the "blue" class:

<a class="vbtn blue">Blue<a>
<button class="vbtn blue">Blue<button>

Blue - Heavy

You can make a button blue and heavy font weight by adding the "heavy" class:

<a class="vbtn blue heavy">Blue<a>
<button class="vbtn blue heavy">Blue<button>

Blue - Large

You can make a button blue and large by adding the "large" class:

<a class="vbtn blue large">Blue<a>
<button class="vbtn blue large">Blue<button>

Blue - Mini

You can make a button blue and miniature by adding the "mini" class:

<a class="vbtn blue mini">Blue<a>
<button class="vbtn blue mini">Blue<button>

Blue - Flat

You can make a button blue and appear flat by adding the "flat" class:

<a class="vbtn blue flat">Blue<a>
<button class="vbtn blue flat">Blue<button>
